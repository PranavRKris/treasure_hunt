Artefact III — The Celestial Papyrus (Segment 3 · 3 digits)

A star chart was found rolled inside the canopic jar. Three ancient beasts are mapped across the sky — each beast is drawn as a constellation. The stars that form each beast, reading from west to east. The three counts, written together, form the third seal.

WEST BEAST CENTRE BEAST EAST BEAST
"Three beasts keep watch over the vault. The western creature stands alone. The eastern creature mirrors it. Only the central beast draws lines between its stars — count them all. Read west to east; concatenate the three counts."
Each colour on the star chart corresponds to one beast / constellation. Count how many stars of each colour exist: gold (west), blue (centre), orange (east). Write the three counts together — no spaces, no separators — to form a 3-digit number. That is your third segment.
